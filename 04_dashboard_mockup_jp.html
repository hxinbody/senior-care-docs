체조 히트맵AICall_HealthCheckRecordGeneralCondition, RecordDate (주별 집계)

인지기능 추이AICall_CognitiveScreenTotalScore, DelayedRecallScore, VerbalFluencyCount, TrendDirection
통화 이력AICall_SessionScenarioType, SummaryText, CallDurationSec, CallStatus
